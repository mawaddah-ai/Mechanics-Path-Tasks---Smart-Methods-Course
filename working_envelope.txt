
 Third: Working Envelope Elements 

| Element                | Value / Description                                  
|------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------
| Reach Radius           | Up to 1.5 meters for the robotic arm.               
| Working Height         | From ground level up to 2.2 meters (for high shelving). 
| Rotation Angle         | 360° base rotation; 180° per joint in the arm.      
| Movement Speed         | Around 1 m/s (depending on floor type).             
| Maximum Payload        | 10–15 kg per load.                                  
| Positioning Accuracy   | ±1 cm during picking and placing tasks.             
| Field of Vision        | 120° camera view + 360° sensor coverage.
